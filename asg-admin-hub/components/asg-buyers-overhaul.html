Buyer FAQ

The questions buyers actually ask.

Do I pay anything to work with a buyer's agent? +

In most Chicago transactions, the seller's listing agreement covers buyer-side compensation. We'll review the specifics in your buyer agreement before you sign anything — no hidden fees, ever.

How fast can I see a home once I find one I like? +

Same day in most cases. We tour aggressively in the first 48 hours of a listing because the best Chicago homes move fast.

Do you have a preferred lender? +

We work with several. We'll introduce you to two or three based on your situation and let you compare — we don't take referral fees.

What's the typical timeline from first showing to closing? +

For most cash-strong buyers, 30–45 days from offer accepted to keys. For first-time buyers using financing, plan for 45–60.
