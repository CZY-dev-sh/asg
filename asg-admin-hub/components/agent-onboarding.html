ASG Agent Onboarding

New Agent Onboarding

A simple launch checklist for getting a real estate agent ready with admin, compliance, brand, tech, marketing, and first-month training.

Agent Intake

Launch Checklist

  • Confirm license, brokerage paperwork, W-9, and compliance requirements.
  • Create Compass, Google Drive, calendar, CRM, and shared tool access.
  • Collect headshot, bio, contact info, social links, testimonials, and brand assets.
  • Build agent folder, email signature, business card, and public profile assets.
  • Set up CRM pipeline, lead sources, follow-up cadence, and saved views.
  • Prepare launch announcement, social templates, email blast, and website/microsite copy.
  • Review buyer process, seller process, deal tracker, offer workflow, and closing timeline.
  • Schedule weekly coaching, first open house plan, and 30-day pipeline review.

First 30 Days

Days 1-2

Set Up

Kickoff, compliance, systems access, folder setup, bio/headshot collection.

Week 1

Brand + Tools

Email signature, CRM, landing page, marketing templates, buyer/seller materials.

Week 2

Training

Lead response, buyer consult, listing prep, offer strategy, deal operations.

Weeks 3-4

Launch

Sphere campaign, prospecting blocks, open house plan, coaching, pipeline review.
