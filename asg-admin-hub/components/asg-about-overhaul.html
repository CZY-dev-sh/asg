Our Story

Alex Stoykov founded ASG with a simple frustration: the buying and selling experience felt unnecessarily complicated, and most agents were too busy to actually advise.

A decade and 800+ closed transactions later, the team is built around the same principle. Every client gets a senior agent's strategic attention, a clear plan, premium marketing, and disciplined negotiation. Nothing flashy for its own sake — just real results.

We're a small team by design. We choose our clients the same way we hope clients choose us — carefully — so we can stay deeply involved from first conversation through final close, and into the years that follow.
