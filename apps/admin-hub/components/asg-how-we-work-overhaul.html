How We Work

A clear plan, every step written down.

The same workflow we use across every transaction — refined over a decade, tightened with every closing.

01

Phase 1 · Week 0

Strategy Session

A 45–60 minute conversation — in person or by video. We map your goals, timeline, must-haves vs. nice-to-haves, and surface the constraints we'll need to design around. You walk out with a custom plan and a written list of what happens next.

What we cover

Goals, budget or comp range, timing, market snapshot, expectations.

What you leave with

A written strategy, recommended next steps, and lender or attorney intros if you need them.

02

Phase 2 · Week 1–3

Preparation

For sellers: staging direction, pre-list repairs, professional media (photo, video, drone, 3D, floor plans), copywriting, pricing analysis. For buyers: lender pre-approval, hot-list curation, neighborhood deep-dives, tour calendar.

For sellers

Stage, fix, photograph, write, price.

For buyers

Pre-approval, hot list, tour schedule.

03

Phase 3 · Week 3–6

Negotiation

Every offer reviewed with comps, days-on-market context, and seller motivation analysis. We structure terms for leverage — price, timing, contingencies — and walk away when the numbers don't work.

Data first

Recent closings, active inventory, absorption rate.

Strategy second

Escalation, contingencies, timing leverage.

Discipline last

When the math says walk, we walk.

04

Phase 4 · Week 6–10

Close & Beyond

Inspection responses, attorney review, appraisal coordination, walk-through, closing day. Then the hand-off — utilities, mover intros, neighborhood onboarding. We stay reachable for the next decade, not the next quarter.
